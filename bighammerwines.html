THE CHALLENGE

Critical Operations Gap After Team Departure

Complete Team Loss

The entire web development department left unexpectedly, leaving zero technical coverage for the business's online operations and critical e-commerce platform.

Manual Processes

Countless repetitive tasks left behind consumed hours of daily work across multiple departments, with no automation or streamlined workflows in place.

Maintenance Backlog

Growing list of technical issues, feature requests, and optimization needs with no resources to address them, impacting customer experience and sales.

Cross-Department Support Gap

Multiple departments relied on technical support for daily operations, with no dedicated resource to handle urgent requests or provide technical guidance.

Critical Business Impact

Without immediate technical leadership, the business faced potential revenue loss, declining customer satisfaction, operational inefficiencies, and inability to execute marketing campaigns or seasonal promotions effectively.

THE SOLUTION

End-to-End Technical Operations Takeover

Stepped in to independently manage all technical operations, implementing AI-driven solutions and comprehensive automation to not just maintain operations, but significantly improve them.
